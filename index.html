The idea!

The idea comes cause I feel lazy to go out of the bed to shut-off the lights in the bedroom before going to sleep.
Then this idea become real after hard work, hard code of software, read tons of information, play with hardware and expend a lot of money to get the system work.

At this point the system is capable of control multiple smart switches, get sensors information, control air quality and more features are coming soon.

Software Version: 2.0
Hardware Version: 1 Rev. 03


Hardware and Software Solution fully developed by Francisco Calero, just for Fun.

Security in Mind

Out there are many options to play with, but I freak out to find how much of my information is shared (stolen in many cases).
So for that I developed my own solution wich makes me safe that nobody get my data, metada or usage records (not even me!).
There are not records, time stamps or something related that can be used to track you down, the only store information is your APP settings wich is local store inside the APP, delete the APP and bye bye records that simple.

Get online

Give you the freedom to choose. You can make it work over the internet using your own server, a free or paid service as www.no-ip.com at ease or can use in the safety of your private network setting alarms to auto control what you need.
